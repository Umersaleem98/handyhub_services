At Zariya, we don't just sell sarees; we preserve a dying art. Our master weavers in Varanasi spend up to 45 days crafting a single piece, using techniques passed down through seven generations.

Each motif tells a story—of peacocks dancing in rain, of temple architecture, of royal courts. When you wear Zariya, you wear history.
